About

ReviewMe is a place to get your projects reviewed as you learn. Our projects are designed to help you utilise the resources already out there on the internet and put them into practice. We encourage curiosity, self-learning and getting hands on with our practical projects.

Our dedicated team of reviewers are passionate to help you learn, and will provide personalised feedback on your work in order to help you progress further as you learn new concepts and skills.

Learn, build & get reviewed on your projects with us at ReviewMe.

You can always get in touch with us at support@reviewme.io if you have any questions, feedback or would love to have a chat!
