Our Privacy Commitment

This Privacy Policy explains how ReviewMe collects and uses personally identifiable information of visitors of our web site and users of our products and services. We recognise the important trust you are placing in us through providing us with your private information. We commit to doing everything we can to maintain that trust.

Collection

We collect your name and email address and any other information you give to us in order to communicate with you (including responding to any questions you may have and providing you with our service). We do not disclose your information to any third party organizations. All information collected will be used solely within ReviewMe in order to provide you with the best experience.

Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. Our measures which are in place are reasonably designed to help protect your personally identifiable information from unauthorised access.

However, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never be able to defeat these measures or use your personally identifiable information for improper purposes. Therefore, you should be aware that when you provide us personally identifiable information -that information could be collected and used by others. We are not responsible for the unauthorized use by third parties of information you post or otherwise make available to us.
