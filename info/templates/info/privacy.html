Our Privacy Commitment

This privacy policy explains how ReviewMe collects and uses personally identifiable information. We recognise the important trust you are placing in us through providing us with your private information. We commit to doing everything we can to maintain that trust.

Collection

We collect your name and email address and any other information you give to us in order to use ReviewMe. All information collected will be used solely within ReviewMe in order to provide you with the best experience. This may include us contacting you regarding enquiries you have made, general support or asking for your feedback.

Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. Our measures which are in place are reasonably designed to help protect your personally identifiable information from unauthorised access.

However, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never be able to defeat these measures or use your personally identifiable information.
